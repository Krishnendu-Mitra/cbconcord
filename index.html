Experience the oldest fest

Concord, the largest fest organised by the United Students’ Organization of the Calcutta Boys’ School is an annual celebration that encapsulates the vibrant spirit and diversity of our student community. It serves as a platform for students from every school to showcase their talents, creativity, and skills across various domains, including academics, arts, sports, and cultural activities.

35+ events

Are you the next Picasso? Or the next Raghu Rai? Or the next Wordsworth? Want to find out? Welcome you to Concord 2025 then. We bring you 35+ events, hence buckle up to express your inner artists, poets, photographers and much more, and rise high along with the Phoenix!

5000+ audience

Want to witness the flooding spirits of our youth? Want to embrace its energy? Want to be a part of unrestrained enthusiasm? Set your calendars to 3rd, 4th and 5th of July to catch the dynamic youth of Kolkata in its own glory at Concord 2025.

25+ schools

If you love competition, camaraderie, and harmonious rivalry, be sure not to skip Concord 2025 at any cost, because 25+ schools from all across Kolkata with thousands of participants of immense talent and vigour will come together to contend against each other. Don’t miss the chance to witness this enormous talent pool at Concord 2025!
